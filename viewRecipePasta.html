Delicious Pasta Primavera

A colorful, fresh, and delicious pasta dish loaded with seasonal vegetables and a light creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1 Cup Mixed Vegetables (Bell Peppers, Zucchini, Carrots)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic (Minced)
  • 1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta as per package instructions and drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and sauté.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and parmesan cheese, mix well.
  5. Add cooked pasta, season with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir well and serve hot. Enjoy!
