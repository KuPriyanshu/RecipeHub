Classic Margherita Pizza

A simple yet flavorful pizza topped with fresh basil, mozzarella, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza Dough
  • 1/2 Cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Cup Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220¬∞C (425¬∞F).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella slices and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden brown.
  7. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy!
